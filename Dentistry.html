About The School

Sree Balaji Dental College & Hospital, Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research functions with objectives of providing accessible, affordable and quality Dental Education and Oral Health Care to the society.

The Dental College and Hospital was established in the year 1989 with an annual intake of 40 students and currently the annual intake is 100 students.

Sree Balaji Dental College & Hospital is one of the premier educational institutions offering quality and affordable Dental care in India. The Dental College has excellent infrastructure with over 1,75,000 sft. of built up area and is the largest of its kind in Tamil Nadu. It has well equipped state-of-the-art library, modern clinical departments and pre-clinical and clinical laboratories. There is free transport facility for the patients and the treatment is also provided free of cost for the deserving poor.

The Dental College has been given the highest score among all dental colleges in the state by the High Level Committee appointed by Government of Tamil Nadu. Our college has gained the students prestigious position of being recognized internationally. The college was made to provide education to poor people who can't afford to pay much money for education in high standard schools. The college was established in the year 1989. Its main motive was to spread knowledge to people of all sections and for excellent education of the candidates, library and hostels was established along with the college. The college started achieving success gradually and excellent result of students in that time gave wings to success.

Sree Balaji Dental College offers one of the most forward-looking educational environments in dental medicine in the country. Located in the capital of Tamilnadu, Chennai, the college is dedicated to training doctors of dental medicine as expert clinicians with strong biomedical backgrounds.

At Sree Balaji Dental College, research and teaching are complementary, producing professionals who possess the analytical capacity and the spirit of inquiry to deal with an ever-increasing knowledge base and rapid technological changes.

By treating patients in the college's teaching clinics, Sree Balaji Dental College students understand not just how to apply science to practice, but how to employ compassion in the treatment of human illness.

Sree Balaji Dental College recently celebrated 25 years of service to education and humanity. With a faculty of dedicated clinicians, and researchers of which many have earned solid reputations as experts in their fields, the Dental school offers a full spectrum of under graduate and postgraduate specialty programs.

An outstanding roster of dental professionals, and dental specialists trained in areas such as Oral and Maxillo Facial surgery, Preventive and community dentistry, conservative dentistry and Endodontics, Oral Pathology, Orthodontics and dental facial orthopedics, Prothodontics, Periodontics and Oral Medicine, help students to attain proficiency in different dental specialties.

Sree Balaji Dental College & Hospital has a blend of experienced, knowledgeable and dedicated team of professionals who are very competent and highly qualified. The staff are constantly engaged in guiding the students towards acquiring expertise in both theory & clinical aspects of Dental and basic medical sciences.

Quality education is imparted with the latest technical know-how. The staff, to be adept in knowledge and practical skills and also to inculcate the basic virtues of service, punctuality and discipline, grooms the students. Sree Balaji Dental College & Hospital conducts frequent dental health camps for educating, motivating and treating the general public, especially the rural population, so as to improve their oral hygiene and health status.

The College presently offers Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S) Course of five-year duration Programme and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) Courses in the specialties of

  • Prosthodontics
  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery
  • Orthodontics
  • Periodontics
  • Conservative Dentistry
  • Pedodontics
  • Oral Pathology
  • Oral Medicine

The College is in constant pursuit of dental health care for the public, following the highest ethical standards.

The college is a premier institute for research, under Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research. The dental college has its own exclusive Central Research Facility, which is open for in house researchers, exchange research scholars, PhD scholars, Post Graduate and Under Graduate Students.

All our academic staff members are based in Departments or Institutes within our Schools, providing them with a network of colleagues. Linking across these bases are interdisciplinary Centres and Institutes recognized by the College. These encourage research with thematic foci and/ or methodological approaches that cannot be contained within any single discipline or department, particularly by offering opportunities for researchers from different Departments to come together for debate and co-work.

Research Centre in Medical Human Genetics & COCPAR : Centre Of Excellence For Oral Cancer Prevention Awareness & Research are the specialty Research centers of Sree Balaji Dental College & Hospital which houses state of the art research facility.
